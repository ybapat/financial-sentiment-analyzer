Frequently Asked Questions

What does Listening Trader do?

It analyzes Reddit comments for stock and crypto tickers, applies AI sentiment analysis, and visualizes the results in real time.

How often is the data updated?

Data is refreshed every hour by an automated backend script.

Is my data private?

Yes. The app only analyzes public Reddit data and does not collect any personal information.

Can I contribute or suggest features?

Absolutely! Visit the GitHub repo to contribute or open issues.

Is this financial advice?

No. This app is for informational and educational purposes only. Always do your own research before investing.
